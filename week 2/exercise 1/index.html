Thomas J Watson Sr.

Introduction

Thomas Jhon Watson Sr. (February 17, 1874 - June 19, 1956) was an american buisnessmann who worked for IBM. He was the chairman and CEO of IBM. He proposed the famous motto "Think" in company offices. He rised from being a son of lumber dealer to the head of one of the most important companies in the world. From his first job as a book keeper, selling sewing machines and musical instruments to being the head of IBM he worked his way to the top. He created motto and slogan which are famous in the buisness world.
  1. "Think" a motto to motivate the workers at IBM now is the symbol of IBM
  2. "World Peace Through World Trade" this slogan was created with deep intrest to international trade
